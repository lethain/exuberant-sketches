About this site

January 1, 2021

I’m Will Larson, and I’ve been writing on my blog, Irrational Exuberance for over a decade, in addition to a couple longer form writing projects, Staff Engineer and An Elegant Puzzle. As more folks have started to read my work, I’ve been thinking about how to find a home for the sorts of content that I enjoy writing but which are too unedited and rough to share with readers who expect writing to be written for them as opposed for for me.

This is Exuberant Sketches, my digital garden, where I write and collect random things without any attention to readers.

I’ll continue to write edited, structured content on Irrational Exuberance.
